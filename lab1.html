About Me

Hi, My name is Jieru(Kathy)! I’m currently pursuing my Master in Human-Computer Interaction at UC Berkeley, and my focus has been on building digital products that enhance user experience at scale. For example, at Pfizer, I launched an AI-powered outreach platform that enabled sales teams to reach over 700 previously disengaged doctors by delivering highly personalized, compliant content. And at Cancer Patient Lab, I led the end-to-end development of a healthcare chatbot that supports over 400 daily active users, giving patients more timely and trustworthy answers. Across these projects, what’s been consistent is my ability to understand complex user pain points, translate them into strategic product solutions, and drive measurable impact at scale.

My Projects

Project 1: AI-powered sales outreach platform at Pfizer

When I joined Pfizer, we were preparing for the ASCO annual meeting, but I noticed a major problem — 75% of doctors were disengaged, and reps could only reach about 80 doctors in five days by sending content manually. To fix this, I launched an AI-powered outreach platform with two key features: automation to let reps scale campaigns, and a matching algorithm to make sure each doctor received content aligned to their research. In our MVP tests, coverage expanded dramatically and click-through rates improved by 15%, while reps rated usability 89 out of 100. After we scaled, over 300 reps adopted the tool, reaching essentially all their assigned doctors and signing up more than 700 new ones, which boosted pre-funnel conversions by 12%. The big takeaway for me was learning how to go from problem discovery to full launch, balancing automation with personalization and driving adoption through training and feedback loops.

Project 2: Healthcare Chatbot at Start-up

When I was a technical product manager at Cancer Patient Lab, I led the end-to-end development of an LLM + RAG based healthcare chatbot. Our goal was to give patients immediate, trustworthy answers to questions about their diagnosis, treatment options, and coping strategies. Within 2 months, we scaled to over 400 daily active users, supporting 10,000 conversations where patients found crucial answers.
